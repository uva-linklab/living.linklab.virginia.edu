Sensors

The Living Link Lab is instrumented with a range of sensors for longitudinal data collection.

Installed

Temperature & Humidity

Each room and common space is instrumented with a temperature and humidity sensors. These take readings every five minutes normally, and more frequently if the measurements change significantly.

Door Sensors

These sensors detect whether the door is open or closed, and are installed on all doors in the Link Lab.

Occupancy

Certain rooms are outfitted with motion-based occupancy sensors that estimate if a room is occupied or not.

Air Quality

Several air quality sensors distributed across the Link Lab will measure CO2, VOCs, and PM2.5.

Planned

Water Usage

Water flow meters will measure the total amount of domestic water used in the Link Lab. Additionally, we expect to submeter the bathroom and kitchen areas.

Circuit-level Electricity

Every circuit in the Link Lab will be instrumented and provide at least minute-level energy use resolution.

Plug-load Electricity

We plan to instrument plug loads in the Link Lab. We are provisioning to instrument every load, but privacy and practical concerns will make the deployment more limited in practice.

Location

A deployment of Estimotes will provide approximate location data for occupants in the Link Lab.

Sensors

The Living Link Lab is instrumented with a range of sensors for longitudinal data collection.

Installed

Temperature & Humidity

Each room is instrumented with a sensor. They take readings every five minutes or when there is a significant change. Details

Door Sensors

These sensors detect whether the door is open or closed, and are installed on all doors in the Link Lab. Details

Light Level Sensors

These sensors measure and report the amount of available light in the room. Details

Occupancy

Certain rooms are outfitted with motion-based occupancy sensors that estimate if a room is occupied or not. Details

Air Quality

Several air quality sensors distributed across the Link Lab will measure CO2, VOCs, and PM2.5. Details

Plug-load Electricity

Plug loads have been deployed throughout the Link Lab to measure energy usage for devices such as printers and computers. Details

Location

A deployment of Estimotes provides approximate location data for occupants in the Link Lab. Details

Planned

Water Usage

Water flow meters will measure the total amount of domestic water used in the Link Lab. Additionally, we expect to submeter the bathroom and kitchen areas.

Circuit-level Electricity

Every circuit in the Link Lab will be instrumented and provide at least minute-level energy use resolution.
